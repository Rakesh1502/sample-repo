Vel Tech University, Chennai

The School pioneers world-class centre of excellence in education and Research through collaborative. consultative and participatory approaches to nurture effective capabilities for the development of high quality technical and scientific manpower to meet the challenges of the knowledge era. The sustained effort of the School is to produce graduates with potential to design and develop systems involving the integration of software and hardware devices; innovative approaches to programming and problem solving as well as creative ways to use computers
